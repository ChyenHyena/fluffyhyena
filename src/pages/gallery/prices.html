    TOS:

  • At the moment I only work SFW and nudity, sorry. Suggestive can be discussed. Won't do sexual content or fetish.

  • Detailed outfits/armor/wings/scales/etc might carry extra fees.

  • I can work from either text descriptions or picture ref, but in the latter case please make sure it is of a high-enough resolution to see the details.

  • You'll be contacted multiple times during the process with WIPs for you to approve.

  • You can ask for modifications up to 4 times without extra fees. Unless it's a mistake/oversight of my own, in which case it's always for free.

  • I reserve the right to post a low-res version of the image on my portfolios/galleries/examples sheet.

  • Starting at $20, half of the payment will be upfront. The rest after the commission is finished.

  • Refunds are only for extreme circumstances (i.e. broken tablet) and only if the commission has still not been finished. There are no refunds for finished pieces.

  • Unless otherwise specified, image format will be .png.

  • Paypal only.
